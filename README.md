# FoodTrends: Understanding Customer Preferences in Food & Beverage

## Project Overview

FoodTrends is a data-driven internship project focused on analyzing customer preferences in the food and beverage domain. The project aims to identify patterns in dietary habits, nutritional intake, and their impact on health and lifestyle.

By leveraging data analysis techniques, this project provides meaningful insights into how food choices vary among individuals and how these choices influence overall well-being. The outcomes can help in making informed decisions related to diet planning, health awareness, and food recommendations.

## Objectives

* To analyze customer dietary preferences and consumption patterns
* To study the relationship between nutrients and common diseases
* To provide personalized and data-driven breakfast suggestions
* To evaluate the impact of lifestyle and nutrition on health
* To generate actionable insights for better food and health decisions

## Project Milestones & Detailed Analysis
## 1. Dietary Preferences and Averages
![image alt]
([https://github.com/kumarithoka/FoodTrends/blob/de7990250688e35c1a2b38527165a891a9a32cc0/IMG_20260323_221359.png](https://github.com/kumarithoka/FoodTrends/blob/d23ecfd2dcb9749be9ead72f64c37b4332931691/IMG_20260323_221359.png))
### Focus:
This phase focuses on understanding customer dietary patterns by analyzing ingredient-level nutrition, different types of diets such as vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and vegan, along with cooking behavior and frequency of food consumption, in order to evaluate how these factors influence overall calorie and nutrient intake.

### Data Considered:
The analysis is based on user dietary preferences, daily food consumption records, and ingredient usage patterns, which together provide a clear view of how individuals structure their meals and maintain their diet.

### Analysis Performed:
In this stage, average calorie intake is calculated across different diet types, followed by comparative analysis to identify variations between groups, along with trend analysis to determine the most commonly consumed ingredients and dietary habits.

### Key Insights:
The analysis reveals that a majority of users follow specific dominant dietary patterns, with noticeable variation in calorie intake among different groups, while individuals who cook frequently at home tend to maintain better nutritional balance, and certain ingredients consistently appear as preferred choices across datasets.

### Business/Health Impact:
These insights help in designing targeted food recommendations and improving awareness about maintaining a balanced diet, which can support both individual health goals and food industry decision-making.

## 2. Nutrients and Diseases
![image alt](https://github.com/kumarithoka/FoodTrends/blob/06dd23346181cd90214dbc1a27419dd66a6c930d/IMG_20260323_221433.png)

### Focus:
This milestone concentrates on examining the relationship between nutrient intake and health conditions by analyzing macronutrients such as proteins, carbohydrates, and fats, as well as micronutrients like vitamins and minerals, to understand how imbalances contribute to disease development.

### Data Considered:
The study utilizes data related to nutrient intake levels, recorded health conditions, and lifestyle indicators, which together help in identifying patterns between diet and disease occurrence.

### Analysis Performed:
The analysis involves correlation techniques to establish relationships between nutrients and diseases, along with identifying patterns of nutrient deficiency or excess, and evaluating risk factors associated with unhealthy dietary habits.

###  Key Insights:
The findings indicate that excessive intake of sugar and fats is strongly associated with lifestyle diseases, while deficiencies in essential nutrients contribute to weakened immunity and other health issues, and maintaining a balanced nutrient intake significantly reduces the risk of chronic conditions.

###  Business/Health Impact:
This phase provides valuable insights for preventive healthcare strategies and supports the development of nutrition-based recommendations aimed at improving overall public health.

 ## 3. Averages by Breakfast Suggestions
 ![image alt](https://github.com/kumarithoka/FoodTrends/blob/b799c01022c8425f2c39129668bc6f37570fb1b3/IMG_20260323_221657.png)

### Focus:
This stage focuses on analyzing breakfast consumption habits by evaluating the nutritional distribution of morning meals, identifying gaps in commonly consumed foods, and generating balanced breakfast suggestions based on average intake values.

### Data Considered:
The analysis includes breakfast food items, their nutritional composition, and user eating habits, which collectively help in understanding the quality and adequacy of morning meals.

### Analysis Performed:
Average nutrient values for breakfast are calculated, followed by identification of missing or insufficient nutrients, and formulation of recommendations that align with balanced dietary requirements.

### Key Insights:
The results show that many users either skip breakfast or consume meals with low nutritional value, particularly lacking in protein, while a balanced breakfast significantly contributes to improved energy levels and productivity throughout the day, and simple dietary adjustments can enhance overall nutrition.

### Business/Health Impact:
These insights enable the creation of personalized meal planning strategies and promote healthier daily routines, especially by emphasizing the importance of a nutritious breakfast.

## 4. Life & Nutrition Impact
![image alt](https://github.com/kumarithoka/FoodTrends/blob/6e5755dfec1439af0bbdf7ee926d79c355d0df9b/IMG_20260323_221745.png)

### Focus:
This phase explores the combined impact of lifestyle habits and nutrition on overall health by analyzing factors such as physical activity levels, daily routines, and long-term behavioral patterns to understand their influence on energy and well-being.

### Data Considered:
The study incorporates data related to activity levels, daily schedules, food habits, and health indicators, providing a comprehensive view of how lifestyle and diet interact.

### Analysis Performed:
Pattern recognition techniques are used to compare active and inactive individuals, followed by evaluating the effects of dietary habits on energy levels and long-term health outcomes.

### Key Insights:
The analysis highlights that individuals with active lifestyles tend to maintain better nutritional balance, while poor dietary and lifestyle habits lead to reduced energy and productivity, and a strong connection exists between consistent healthy routines and improved long-term well-being.

### Business/Health Impact:
This stage supports the development of lifestyle-based recommendations and encourages a holistic approach to health improvement by combining proper nutrition with active living.
